Caldereta

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 5 dried bay leaves
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 T whole peppercorns
  • 1 head of garlic, finely chopped
  • 4.6 oz tomato paste
  • 2 T patis (fish sauce)
  • 1 large onion, cut into ½ inch pieces
  • 1 c carrots, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into ½ inch pieces
  • 1 c frozen peas, thawed
  • 1-2 medium potatoes, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 41 - 85g can liver spread
  • 3 slices of American cheese
  • Olives, to your taste
  • Olive oil

Procedure

  • Add cubed beef chuck to a pot and cover with water. Add in peppercorn, bay leaves, salt. Simmer for 45 minutes or until beef is tender. Set aside.
  • In your stew pot/dutch oven, add olive oil over medium-low heat. Saute garlic until just crispy on the edge and pieces start to stick together. Lower the heat to prevent garlic from burning.
  • Add chopped onion and saute for an additional minute.
  • Add tomato paste, beef, and patis/fish sauce. Saute for 3-4 minutes.
  • Add enough beef stock so that the beef is covered by about 1/4 inch. Reserve 1/4 c of broth for later.
  • Add potato and let boil for 5-6 minutes.
  • Add carrot and continue to cook for 15 minutes.
  • At this time, the potato should be pretty tender. Add the bell pepper, beas, and chorizo. Continue to simmer for 10 minutes.
  • In a separate bowl, combine the liver spread with reserved 1/4 beef broth to make a slurry. Add to the stew.
  • Add the 3 slices of cheese (sliced into small pieces) to the stew. Combine until melted. Bring stew to a boil or for a few additional minutes until stew is thickened.
